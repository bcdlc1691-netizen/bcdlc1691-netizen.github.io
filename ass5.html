Esri course and training

To start this assignment, we had to take an Esri course on supervised image classification. Machine learning in GIS

In remote sensing, ML becomes super important as it normally provides a higher accuracy compared to traditional parameters in data classification, especially with many predictor variables. However traditional parametric classifiers are still more common in usage - due to their availability in remote sensing. ML is a field with many uncertainties regarding its use and implementation still, so people seem reluctant. Read more: Maxwell, A. E., Warner, T. A., & Fang, F. (2018). Implementation of machine-learning classification in remote sensing: An applied review. International Journal of Remote Sensing, 39(9), 2784–2817. https://doi.org/10.1080/01431161.2018.1433343

Types of ML in remote sensing include SVMs, (boosted) DTs, RFs, artificial neural networks (ANNs), and k-nearest neighbour (k-NN). These mainly use R to analyze and train for hyperspectral data.
